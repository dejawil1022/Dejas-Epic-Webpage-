Leland

Leland is so misunderstood. Leland is the father of Laura, and takes her death the hardest out of EVERYONE in Twin Peaks (even Laura's own mother, this could be debated tho, and she’s also not on this list, sorry Sarah Palmer). Seeing Leland's and Laura’s character interaction in the movie Fire Walk With Me is both beautiful and quite….strange. He’s a strange man overall, but everytime he came in the shot you know he was gonna do some crazy shit that stirred up the pot. So for that, he gets 9 manic panic virgin snow hair dyes out of 10
